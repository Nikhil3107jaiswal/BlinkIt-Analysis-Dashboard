# Blinkit Sales Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes Blinkit's sales performance, customer satisfaction, and inventory distribution to uncover insights and optimization opportunities using KPIs and Power BI dashboards.

---

## Problem Statement
Blinkit wants to enhance its business by analyzing sales trends, product ratings, and outlet performance. The objective is to deliver key KPIs and visual insights to support data-driven decisions.

---

## Tools & Technologies Used
- Power BI
- Excel
- Git & GitHub

---

## Dataset
Dataset includes:
- Item types and fat content
- Sales data including outlet type, size, and location
- Customer ratings

---

## Approach
1. Cleaned data in Excel
2. Performed EDA to identify patterns
3. Designed calculated columns/measures in Power BI
4. Built interactive dashboards with slicers and visuals
5. Highlighted business KPIs

---

## Key KPIs
- Total Sales: Total revenue generated from all items
- Average Sales: Average revenue per sale
- Number of Items: Total count of items sold
- Average Rating: Customer rating for items sold

---

## Chart Requirements

1. Total Sales by Fat Content
   - Objective: Analyze the impact of fat content
   - Chart: Donut Chart

2. Total Sales by Item Type
   - Objective: Item type-wise sales performance
   - Chart: Bar Chart

3. Fat Content by Outlet for Total Sales
   - Objective: Compare outlets by fat content
   - Chart: Stacked Column Chart

4. Total Sales by Outlet Establishment
   - Objective: How outlet age/type influences sales
   - Chart: Line Chart

5. Sales by Outlet Size
   - Objective: Correlation between size & sales
   - Chart: Donut/Pie Chart

6. Sales by Outlet Location
   - Objective: Geographical distribution
   - Chart: Funnel Map

7. All Metrics by Outlet Type
   - Objective: Complete metrics breakdown
   - Chart: Matrix Card

---

## Results
- Visualized how fat content and item type influence sales
- Found outlet type and size affect revenue
- Identified underperforming product categories and regions


---

## Learnings
- Created multi-layered KPI dashboards
- Used slicers and DAX functions in Power BI
- Improved storytelling using visual elements
